My Awesome Adventure at Epcot & Animal Kingdom

In June 2025, I had the most amazing trip ever with my brother, my mom, and my friends!

Epcot Highlights

At Epcot, we started the day by trying some delicious ice cream—it was so creamy and yummy!

Then we watched “We The Kingdom” live—it was a super fun concert and the music was fantastic!

The “Soarin' Around the World” ride was so cool; it felt like we were flying over famous landmarks!

And of course, we couldn't miss the Avatar ride—it was the best adventure with glowing forests and floating mountains!

Animal Kingdom Thrills

Over at Animal Kingdom, we zoomed down a roller coaster that dropped 200 feet—that was so exciting (and a little scary)!

My favorite was “Guardians of the Galaxy.” I loved it so much, even though we had to wait about two hours to ride. It was totally worth it!

Which Park Was the Best?

It's hard to choose, but I think Epcot was my favorite—it had the tastiest treats, incredible shows, and the coolest rides!

Overall, it was the most fun trip ever, and I can't wait to go back!
